Our Courses

Gordon College offers

COURSES FOR YOU!

Education Courses

Gordon College offers a variety of education courses for aspiring teachers and educators looking to enhance their skills and knowledge in the field of education. These courses cover a range of topics including educational psychology, curriculum development, classroom management, and teaching methods. Students can expect to receive a well-rounded education that prepares them for success in the field of education. With a strong focus on hands-on learning and practical experience, Gordon College's education courses provide students with the tools they need to excel in their future careers as teachers and educators.

Science Courses

At Gordon College, science courses provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the natural world through hands-on laboratory experiences and engaging classroom instruction. Our faculty members are dedicated to facilitating student learning and exploration in subjects such as biology, chemistry, physics, and environmental science. Students have the opportunity to conduct research, participate in field studies, and gain practical skills that prepare them for a wide range of careers in science and technology. With an emphasis on critical thinking and problem-solving, our science courses at Gordon College foster a deep appreciation for the complexities of the world around us.

Mathematics Courses

Gordon College offers a variety of mathematics courses designed to equip students with the analytical and problem-solving skills necessary to excel in a wide range of academic and professional fields. The mathematics department at Gordon College is committed to providing students with a strong foundation in mathematical concepts, as well as opportunities for hands-on application and experiential learning. Whether students are interested in pure mathematics, applied mathematics, or statistics, the mathematics courses at Gordon College aim to develop students' critical thinking abilities and numerical proficiency. With dedicated faculty, small class sizes, and a supportive learning environment, Gordon College's mathematics courses are designed to help students succeed academically and professionally.
